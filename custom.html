Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a custom build typically take?

+

Timeframes vary based on the complexity of your project and our current workload. Simple upgrades may take 1-2 weeks, while comprehensive custom builds can take 4-8 weeks. We'll provide a specific timeline during your consultation.

Do you work with all RC vehicle types?

+

Yes, we work with all RC vehicle types including cars, trucks, buggies, crawlers, and more. We specialize in both electric and nitro-powered vehicles from all major manufacturers.

Can I provide my own parts for the build?

+

Absolutely. We're happy to work with parts you provide. We can also offer recommendations and source high-quality components for your build if you prefer.

Do you offer warranties on custom builds?

+

All our workmanship comes with a 30-day warranty. Individual components typically include manufacturer warranties. Keep in mind that racing and extreme bashing can void certain warranties. We'll discuss warranty details specific to your build during consultation.

Can you ship custom builds internationally?

+

Yes, we ship worldwide. International shipping costs and timeframes will be calculated based on your location and the specifics of your build.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. For custom builds, we typically require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the balance due upon completion.
